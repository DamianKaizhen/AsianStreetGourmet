家

We don't claim to be fancy. We claim to be honest.

Asian Street Gourmet is a small Cantonese family kitchen on a quiet block of Avenue O. The recipes belong to our mother and her mother — steamed rice plates simmered over the same blue flame she used in Guangdong, soups that take all afternoon, fish-ball noodles ladled into paper bowls.

Order by code on the phone, the way it's always been done — "one A1, one C2, two D6" — and we'll have it ready when you arrive. Plates from $7, soups $3. Every plate is made the way home is.

— The family at 84 Avenue O

04 · Often asked

常見問題

Questions, answered.

Q · Where are you?

Asian Street Gourmet is at 84 Avenue O, Brooklyn, NY 11204 — in the Mapleton / Borough Park area.

Q · What are your hours?

Tuesday through Thursday and Sunday, 11am – 9pm. Friday and Saturday, 11am – 10pm. Closed Mondays. Last orders 30 minutes before close.

Q · Do you offer takeout and pickup?

Yes. We're a phone-order, takeout, and dine-in kitchen. Call 917-723-6262 and order by menu code — for example, "one A1, one C2, two D6" — and we'll have it ready for pickup.

Q · How do I order?

Call 917-723-6262 and read off the menu codes and quantities. Each item has a code like A1, B4, or C3. We'll have your order bagged and ready when you arrive.

Q · Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes. Vegetarian choices include the Lotus Root Soup (C1), several vegetable soups, and Fried Mantou sweet buns (D2).

Q · How much does a meal cost?

Steamed rice dishes are $7 to $9, soups and snacks $3 each. An affordable, home-cooked Cantonese kitchen.

Q · Do you take reservations?

We do not take reservations. Orders are placed by phone for pickup, or you can dine in when you arrive.
